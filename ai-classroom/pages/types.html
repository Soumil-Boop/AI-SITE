Types of AI

AI is not one thing. It is a whole family of different technologies, each with its own strengths and purposes.

🤖

Machine Learning

AI systems that learn from data without being explicitly programmed. The more data they see, the smarter they get.

Learning from data
🕸️

Neural Networks

Inspired by the human brain, these are layers of connected nodes that process information. They power face recognition and voice assistants.

Brain-inspired
💬

Natural Language Processing

AI that understands and generates human language. This powers chatbots, translation tools, and AI writing tools.

Language and text
👁️

Computer Vision

AI that can see and interpret images and video. Used in medical imaging, self-driving cars, and facial recognition.

Seeing the world
🎨

Generative AI

AI that creates new content including text, images, music, code, and video. Tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and DALL·E fall into this category.

Creating content
♟️

Reinforcement Learning

AI that learns by trial and error, receiving rewards for good decisions. This is how AI learns to play games and control robots.

Trial and reward
